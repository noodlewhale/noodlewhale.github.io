A2GO

Description:

We created a fun little mobile game based on the pokemon GO formula. This game tracks your GPS location and moves your in game character
as you move around in real life. If you switch modes at a location, the game will change into an Augmented Reality mode. In this mode, you can
plant trees that generate income and fight off the pandas the stunt your tree's growth and income production. For our bonus task, we created a
custom avatar and a tree merging mechanic. Trees can be merged when there are at least 20 trees within a radius of the player. The merged tree
has golden leaves, and grows to be much larger than the other trees. The income it produces is also based on the value of the trees used to make
it, and it is generally a pretty large bonus.
