P1-Cainlab

Description

In this project, a semi accurate representation of the Cain Lab the Beister Building has been modeled and can be walked through with VR.
The representation includes interactabe lightswithes and firealarms that changes the lighting and ambient noise. It also contains trash that can
be either tossed in the bin or duplicated on the multiple copy machines in the lab. If you want to draw, there is a rudimentary whiteboard.
There are also various npcs that you can talk to though they arent too glad to interact with you. If you talk to an npc while the fire alarm
is on, it will trigger a minigame where you have to find 5 buttons within 45 seconds. If you fail, you can always try again, but if you succeed,
you get rewarded with two physics based rocket launchers that can bring mahem to lab.


Technology Usage

  • Unreal Engine 5 - This tool was used to model the cainlab and create all the interactables.
  • Unreal Engine Blueprints - This was our primary tool for programming different functions within the lab.
  • JIRA - Jira was used to assign tasks, stay on schedule, and break down larger issues into digestable tasks.
  • OBS Studio - OBS was used to recorded a preview of our VR environment.
  • GitLab - This is our version control system to share work between different people and store our files.
  • Discord - Main communication tool between group members.
  • YouTube - Main way to share recorded footage of work.
