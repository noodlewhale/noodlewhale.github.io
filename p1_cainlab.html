Technology Usage

  • Unreal Engine 5 - This tool was used to model the cainlab and create all the interactables.
  • Unreal Engine Blueprints - This was our primary tool for programming different functions within the lab.
  • JIRA - Jira was used to assign tasks, stay on schedule, and break down larger issues into digestable tasks.
  • OBS Studio - OBS was used to recorded a preview of our VR environment.
  • GitLab - This is our version control system to share work between different people and store our files.
  • Discord - Main communication tool between group members.
  • YouTube - Main way to share recorded footage of work.
